We are a crowdlending platform that connects verified lenders and borrowers. Because we provide a more efficient service than traditional channels, lenders who are looking for investment opportunities will find attractive returns while borrowers get reasonable rates.

FundKo is a subsidiary of Guevent Investments Development Corporation (GIDC) – a company that has over 50 years of operations with a business footprint that spans across the entire Philippine archipelago.

Invest for as low as 15,000 pesos or borrow as much as 500,000 pesos through FundKo!

How Peer-to-Peer Works

Borrower Applies

We validate the borrower, assess the loan and assign a rate.

Lenders Fund Portions of Loans

Lenders choose from a variety of approved loans in our marketplace.

FundKo Collects and Disburses

FundKo collects repayments from borrowers and distributes returns to lenders
